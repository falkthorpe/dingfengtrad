What we deliver

A complete engineering
surface, end to end.

From programmatic technology and immersive interaction design to fully-published App Store products, we offer a tightly-integrated service stack for European and North-American partners who value privacy and craft.

Service Pillars

Six interlocking
capabilities.

Each pillar is staffed by engineers and designers who specialize in that discipline. They share architecture, processes, and a relentless bar for quality.

Pillar · 01

Programmatic Audio Synthesis

Generative sound engines, procedural ambient design, and on-device DSP pipelines. From meditation textures to adaptive game audio, we craft sound systems that scale.

Pillar · 02

Visual Interaction Design

Motion grammar, micro-interactions, and brand systems that feel inevitable. We design interfaces that pull users into a state of calm, mindful focus.

Pillar · 03

Privacy Engineering

Local-first data architecture, end-to-end encryption, biometric gating, and zero-telemetry defaults. Privacy is not a setting we toggle — it's the foundation.

Pillar · 04

Local Automation Scripts

Workflow engines, scheduled tasks, file processors, and content pipelines that run 100% on device — reliable, auditable, and immune to privacy drift.

Pillar · 05

Creative Capture & Curation

Tools that translate the chaos of inspiration into clear, navigable constellations — text, sketches, voice, links, screenshots — each fragment finds its place.

Pillar · 06

App Store Publication

Full-cycle product launch — store listing optimization, metadata localization, App Review compliance, ASO, and sustainable update cadence for years to come.

Engagement Flow

From first call to shipped product.

A predictable, transparent collaboration. You always know where the project is, what's next, and what to expect.

Week 01 · Discovery

Discovery & Spec Lock

Two scoping sessions, one technical audit, one signed Statement of Work. We emerge with a clear functional spec, prioritized milestones, and a project budget.

Weeks 02–04 · Foundation

Architecture & UX Skeleton

Data model, module boundaries, prototype interactions. A testable build navigable on simulator and device — so feedback is grounded in reality, not abstract decks.

Weeks 05–10 · Build

Iterative Implementation

Bi-weekly drops covering full feature slices. TestFlight betas begin around week 6. Quality gating, accessibility, and performance are continuous, not bolted on.

Week 11–12 · Launch

App Store Submission

Metadata, screenshots, ASO, privacy manifest, ATT labels. Pre-flight against the App Review Guidelines. We shepherd the review and remain on standby for release-day issues.

Months 04+ · Sustain

Steady-State Improvement

Minor upgrades, OS-version alignment, store optimization, and continued hardening. We act as your long-term engineering partner, not a one-off delivery.
