FAQ

Questions we get
often.

What's a typical engagement model?

Either a fixed-scope Statement of Work with milestone billing, or a 6-month retainer for steady-state engineering. Most clients start with a discovery sprint to scope the work.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. Mutual NDAs are standard before any substantive conversation. We treat confidentiality as a feature, not paperwork.

Can you white-label or sub-contract?

Yes for both. Many studios engage us as a stealth back-end engineering partner. IP transfers are negotiated case-by-case.

Do you maintain products you didn't build?

Selected engagements only. We prefer to adopt a codebase we can quickly become the primary authors of, or to be involved from architecture day one.

Which platforms do you ship to?

iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, visionOS, and tvOS. We have partners for Android when a cross-platform release is needed.

How do you handle privacy?

Local data by default. Zero analytics SDKs. Privacy Manifests filed for every App Store release. ATT labels that match the actual behavior. See our Privacy Policy.
